# EPIC-UX-005: Organization & FHIR Entity Management

**Priority:** P0 | **Estimated Effort:** 3 weeks | **Dependencies:** EPIC-UX-001, EPIC-UX-003
**Theme:** Natural Language-Powered Healthcare Resource Management

---

## Executive Summary

This epic covers the management of all core FHIR entities (Organizations, Practitioners, Patients, Locations, Departments) and scheduling resources (Slots, Schedules). Unlike traditional CRUD interfaces, this module leverages the AI Copilot for natural language operations while providing intuitive visual interfaces for browsing, filtering, and bulk operations. The goal is to make administrative tasks feel effortless through voice commands and intelligent automation.

---

## Strategic Objectives

1. **Visual Excellence** - Beautiful list views, detail pages, and data visualization
2. **AI-First Input** - Natural language for all create/update operations
3. **Bulk Operations** - Efficient management of multiple records
4. **Calendar Integration** - Visual roster and slot management
5. **Smart Search** - Instant search with fuzzy matching and filters
