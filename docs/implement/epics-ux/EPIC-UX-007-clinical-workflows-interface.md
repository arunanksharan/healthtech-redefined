# EPIC-UX-007: Clinical Workflows Interface

**Priority:** P0 | **Estimated Effort:** 5 weeks | **Dependencies:** EPIC-UX-001, EPIC-UX-003, EPIC-UX-004
**Theme:** Streamlined Clinical Documentation and Order Management

---

## Executive Summary

This epic delivers the clinical workflow interfaces that healthcare providers use daily: electronic prescriptions (e-Rx), lab orders, imaging orders, referrals, clinical documentation (SOAP notes), vital signs recording, and care planning. The focus is on speed, accuracy, and reducing documentation burden through AI assistance, smart defaults, and voice dictation.

---

## Strategic Objectives

1. **Speed** - Complete common clinical tasks in <30 seconds
2. **Accuracy** - Built-in clinical decision support and drug interaction alerts
3. **AI Assistance** - Voice dictation, auto-completion, and smart suggestions
4. **Integration** - Seamless flow between patient context and orders
5. **Compliance** - HIPAA-compliant, audit-logged, and e-signature ready
