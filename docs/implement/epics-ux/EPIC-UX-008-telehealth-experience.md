# EPIC-UX-008: Telehealth Experience

**Priority:** P0 | **Estimated Effort:** 4 weeks | **Dependencies:** EPIC-UX-001, EPIC-UX-004
**Theme:** Seamless Virtual Care Delivery Platform

---

## Executive Summary

This epic delivers a world-class telehealth experience for both patients and providers. It includes video consultation interfaces, virtual waiting rooms, device checking, screen sharing, e-prescribing integration, and session recording. The platform leverages LiveKit for real-time video and provides a streamlined workflow from appointment booking to post-visit documentation.

---

## Strategic Objectives

1. **Simplicity** - One-click join for patients
2. **Reliability** - Pre-call device checks, fallback options
3. **Integration** - Seamless EHR integration during calls
4. **Documentation** - AI-assisted note-taking during visits
5. **Compliance** - HIPAA-compliant, consent-driven recording
