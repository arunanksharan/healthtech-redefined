# EPIC-UX-009: Billing & Revenue Portal

**Priority:** P1 | **Estimated Effort:** 4 weeks | **Dependencies:** EPIC-UX-001, EPIC-UX-007
**Theme:** Streamlined Revenue Cycle Management Interface

---

## Executive Summary

This epic delivers comprehensive billing and revenue management interfaces for both administrative staff and patients. It includes insurance verification, claims management, patient billing, payment processing, and revenue analytics. The goal is to reduce billing errors, accelerate collections, and provide transparency to patients.

---

## Strategic Objectives

1. **Accuracy** - Real-time eligibility verification and claim validation
2. **Speed** - Automate claim submission and payment posting
3. **Transparency** - Clear patient-facing bills with payment options
4. **Intelligence** - AI-powered denial prediction and management
5. **Compliance** - HIPAA-compliant, audit-ready processes
