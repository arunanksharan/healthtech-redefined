# EPIC-UX-011: Patient Self-Service Portal

**Priority:** P0 | **Estimated Effort:** 4 weeks | **Dependencies:** EPIC-UX-001
**Theme:** Empowering Patients with Digital Healthcare Access

---

## Executive Summary

This epic delivers a comprehensive patient portal enabling self-service access to health records, appointment management, secure messaging with providers, prescription refills, bill payment, and document sharing. The portal is designed for simplicity, accessibility, and mobile-responsiveness.

---

## Strategic Objectives

1. **Self-Service** - Reduce call center load with 24/7 digital access
2. **Engagement** - Increase patient activation and health literacy
3. **Transparency** - Full access to records and visit history
4. **Convenience** - Easy scheduling, messaging, and payments
5. **Accessibility** - WCAG 2.1 AA compliant, multilingual support
